FAQs

Can I earn rewards points at a Kiosk?

Yes. Anytime you use a kiosk you will earn points that can be applied to your account at checkout.

Can I pre-oder using the Starbucks app?

Yes. Simply place your order using our Starbucks Rewards App, and select your closes kiosk. When you arrive scan the QR code provide to complete your checkout.

How do I know when my order it ready?

Non Rewards coustomers will see their drink being prepared on the kiosk display screen. Rewards members will also recieve an in app alert that their drink is ready for pickup.
